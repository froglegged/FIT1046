Description: Directorate personnel were notified about what appeared to be a break-in at the factory district located in the Old Town of K'or. A search revealed the personal items of one Martin Radevic, including a Camcorder, alongside a mobile device at the entrance of Mine #19. Radevic appears lucid but possessed with some unseen purpose driving him forwards. A collection of stills has been collated with transcriptions of Radevic's speech and is available for viewing below.
